FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dental services and care.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a routine checkup and cleaning. However, some patients may need more frequent visits depending on their oral health needs.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes! We accept most major dental insurance plans and will work with you to maximize your benefits. We also offer flexible payment plans for uninsured patients.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Absolutely. Our professional teeth whitening treatments are safe, effective, and supervised by dental professionals. We use medical-grade whitening agents that protect your enamel while delivering dramatic results.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call us immediately! We offer same-day emergency appointments for urgent dental issues such as severe toothaches, broken teeth, or lost fillings. We'll guide you on what to do until you arrive.

Do you offer sedation for anxious patients?

Yes, we offer various sedation options including nitrous oxide (laughing gas) and oral sedation to ensure your comfort. Your anxiety-free experience is our priority.
